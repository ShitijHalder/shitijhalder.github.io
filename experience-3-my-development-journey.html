My Development Journey

Web designers are responsible for designing websites. They use software such as Adobe Photoshop, Sketch, and Figma to create the visual design of a website. They also use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create the front-end of the website and Android developers are responsible for creating applications for devices running the Android operating system. They use a combination of components that can be invoked individually. For example, an activity is a type of app component that provides a user interface (UI). I do both. Mainly Web Designing.

So we all have a dream that we would build something on the internet and we try to learn development. Sometimes it works and sometimes it doesn't works the way we wanted to see it. But what happened with me? What I am doing? What's all going? Why did I start this Coding and Development Journey? How did I get into it? Was I interested in it from the Beginning? To know all of these read my Development Experience now with no distractions. Enjoy.

Welcome to yet another experience post. From the very beginning of my life I was interested in technical things like remote control cars, remote control helicopter, broken electronics, broken headphones and all those things. My Grandfather taught me how to operate electronics and if the electronics gets damaged or stops working somehow then how to fix it. I was very interested in those. I loved it. When I was growing up the urge to play with those things kinda started to lose and by time I left it completely. 2020 came and schools closed, to fill up my time I took part in Android in which I wanted to learn Android Development. I searched on Google - How to make custom rom for this device? In results I got a match of a Telegram link of a Device Community. I created my Telegram account and got straight into it. I logged in and saw the group it, it was huge. Many people were talking about the device (Specific). I sent a text, any custom roms available for this device (Specific)? One user replied and gave me a rom link and I installed the first ever custom rom my device. I was happy. Then I asked how to make one of these? The same user replied and I got into it, I learnt it and created my very own Custom rom. I was very happy about it. I learnt many more things. And after some months I had to left the development beacause I didn't have any servers to operate. I left it. I don't know whether I will be able to do it again or not but let's see.

Read Above?

I also started Web Designing in late 2020s, I have a brother in my contacts who is doing BCA right now and he very pro in Web Designing. I started learning HTML and CSS from Youtube and also took guide from that brother of mine. I am just doing fine in Designing. I created my first ever website and it was so good not gonna lie. I finihed the building of the website in 8 Months I know it's a long time but you know. I learned more and more and more. It was not so easy at first I couldn't understand what to do and how to do. I was a newbie but somehow I managed. Everytime I ran my .js code it showed me error. My CSS was very very fucked up, I launched my website, full of bugs. I click on something and it showed something else. But didn't give up. Recently I redigned the whole website and now it up. I am so happy that I am doing something good at last. Apart from wasting my time and doing nothing at all, I love doing this work. I enjoy it. My journey is still going on and on and on... That's it, I thank you guys for reading and spending your precious time in this post. I really appreciate it. See you in the next post, till then be Kool and be Safe!
