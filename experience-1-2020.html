My 2020 Life

As we all know that 2020 was hell, 2020 was a year that will be remembered for the COVID-19 pandemic, which spread rapidly across the globe and had a profound impact on nearly every aspect of daily life. Governments implemented lockdowns and social distancing measures in an effort to slow the spread of the virus, leading to widespread economic disruption and job losses. Many people were forced to work from home, while schools and universities shifted to online learning. The pandemic also highlighted and exacerbated existing inequalities and injustices.

So apart from China's COVID-19 God Level Virus we all have a personal life right? So you must be thinking that I would have enjoyed 2020 too much at the peak but fuck no I didn't man, seriously I didn't enjoy anything. Why I am saying this? Well That depends. So to get to know better what I am saying go through my experience of 2020, yeah first blog will be {Experience}

It all started with a fine new year and within two months everything was down. I didn't had wifi or a personal phone that time. But with the help of my poor potato pc with AMD A4 and my mom's mobile phone "Cereus" I was somehow living. But I wasn't happy at all you know and that's not because of I wasn't getting anything to play but the actual issue was I had no one to play with and it really killed my brain, it affected me too much that I couldn't even explain that feeling. It was worse than ever. I missed everyone, everyone, even I missed my unworthy relatives, yeah I was that alone. I had no one to share my talk and feeling. I was finally into Depression. Congratulate me when? I was in, and I am still in while writing all these bugged code, nothing to say. All over the news that from next week there will be schools opened, from the next day there is school. And to be honest parents didn't help that much that I am mentally. I was already in Depression and now this stress of school. I wanted to go to school but I was afraid of loads of homework too and I was devastated. No cap man fr. I mean what am I even saying right now? Does that even makes any freaking sense? We all suffered right? It's just that I can't say my feeling to someone in real life or in-person, its really hard to open up about all these. If I start either my friends will laugh and will do a fake condolence and after 69 seconds they won't give a damn. No one cares. No one!

Read Above?

Now this is what I was thinking all the year of 2020 and yeah its not the end, there is more. I was, I am thinking about all these things in my freaking head. I really wish about having a switch off button, that whenever I need I would switch it off and turn it back on again after some time, it's frustating, why it wouldn't be? Its the only lazy and useless part of human body. I am a Biology student, I can assure it. No cap fr. Trust me I am truth.
