Fake Friends

Tags : Fake Friendship Overacting No Faith No Trust No Management Toxic Friendship

Fake Friendship

Childhood friends hold a special place in a person's life. Growing up together creates a bond and shared experiences that can last a lifetime. These friendships can provide a sense of security, familiarity, and important life lessons. As people mature, childhood friendships may change or fade away, but with effort and communication, they can endure. Maintaining these relationships can be challenging but can be incredibly rewarding. Childhood friends can offer comfort, support, and a connection to important memories and experiences..

We all have friends right or no? Tell man! So it goes like this I got a friend group.. we were 4 including me, we grew up together and in the middle of nowhere everything got changed without any notice. We all 4 didn't even expect that this could happen in our lives. For a second we all were "Yeah man we all up, Let's do some shits and all and lesssss gooo" and the 2nd second was like everything changed. If you wanna know what I am talking about then experience my experience without no ad breaks yeah I didn't put any ads cause I want you guys to get a smooth and stable experience. Now enjoy.

So welcome back and yeah good morning good evening good night whatever you say whatever you like I don't care and yeah this is the second block post I mean this is my experience post. So intoday's experience post is about my friends and you know what they are pretty good seriously I am not joking they are pretty good so I joined my school in class 3 before I was in Delhi okay and I did study there for 3 years I mean yeah 2.5 years so yeah I got four friends in class 3 I won't tell the name but my friends would see the post I guess so they will know and yeah they will talk about it later with me so let's start so I got four friends in class 3 and they are good pretty good they help me they were good I would just say they are good, I would say growing up with them was pretty good I mean it was so good I had a good experience. My childhood was great, yes my childhood was great with them I could not know about time, and yeah in the meantime, time flew I don't know what I am saying but yeah time flew and we didn't even realize it. So in class 8 okay? In class 8 we were at the peak we would say that bro listen I know everything about you, okay I know everything about this world, people are bad, and they are good but only some. So one of my friends of that 4 was toxic, toxic in the sense he did not fit in the group growing up I mean he was good but to be honest I didn't like him so much and yeah I would say that he did not fit at all okay his hairstyle, talking accent, habits, and his other friends were all in a very bad influence and everything was imperfect I would say wait is there a word imperfect I don't know if that word exists lmao, continue so the all four helpful they helped me with my works and shit with everything...We all got promoted to class 10 okay yes class 10 I remember we all got promoted at the same time and yeah we didn't score much okay we didn't unfortunately and we had to wait for nearly about 10 days just to get admission in our school because there were no seats available in our school, but remember I was talking about that guy who didn't fit in our-group? He scored the 2 lowest among us I don't remember clearly but yeah he got admission to the Humanities (Arts), he wanted to be in Commerce he couldn't get into Commerce.. he took a lot of time deciding whether to be in this school or change this school because wanted to be an Army Man and luck Didn't fall for him and we heard that he left our school and joined another private school and there he got Commerce. I was sad, really I was feeling bad about this thing and not just that he left the school but he broke our group. He just broke out of friendship without saying anything. We 3 were left. I got into the Science section, my 2nd friend got into the Commerce section and my 3rd friend got into the Humanities section. After that, we didn't get much time to talk or hang out. We all three were in the same school that's what matters. We get time in recess and we have lunch together and hang out, you know the term 'Yaari' so yeah that's it. The day before yesterday I got a call from my 'that friend' and I picked up, and prepared myself for the talk, I thought that he will first say Hi or something but he straight got to the point and he started talking about some admission form and all that, the conversation did go for 22 seconds, now you can imagine what he talked about and I hope he changes in the future, wherever he is right now I hope he is good. I miss him somewhere you know 'Bachpan ki Dosti'? That's what it is. That's it. Done for today and yeah I just wanna add my friends were, are, and will be a blessing for me. Fuckin love you guys (No homo nigga) now bye!
