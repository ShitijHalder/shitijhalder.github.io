Some Words About Me

My Awesome Story

Embarking on the journey of web development in late 2020, I had been gearing up for this adventure since 2018. The road was filled with countless learning opportunities and sources of inspiration. Although I didn't have a personal mentor, YouTube became my constant companion, guiding me through the complexities of development. I dedicated approximately nine months to studying the intricacies of website creation, and by the end of 2021, I successfully published my website. However, life, as it often does, threw a curveball my way. I stumbled during my finals and found myself at a standstill. Coding, once my daily routine, became a distant memory as months passed without a single line of code written. Despite the setback, I refused to let disappointment cloud my vision. Motivated to improve and grow, I picked up where I left off, determined to shape a better future. Today, I stand at this juncture of my journey, living in the present and doing exactly what destiny has outlined for me in the divine script. Life, with its ups and downs, is treating me well. Like a fine wine, I am maturing with time, getting better with each passing day.
